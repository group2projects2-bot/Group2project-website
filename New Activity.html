What is STVEP?

The Strengthened Technical-Vocational Program (STVEP) is an initiative by the Philippine Department of Education aimed at enhancing the delivery of technical-vocational education in secondary schools. STVEP stand for Strengthened Technical-Vocational Education Program, designed to provide students with practical skills and comprehensions aligned with industry standards. It supports the K to 12 Basic Education Curriculum and integrates Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE) subjects.

Key Features

  • Inudstry-Revelant Training
  • Hands-on Experience/Learning
  • Curriculum Integration
  • Pathway to employment or entrepreneurship
